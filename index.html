Yu Yang

I am an Assistant Professor with the School of Data Science at City University of Hong Kong. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computing Science from Simon Fraser University, where I was supervised by Prof. Jian Pei. Before that, I obtained my M.E. from University of Science and Technology of China in 2013, and my B.E. from Hefei University of Technology in 2010, both in Computer Science.

My research interests lie in the algorithmic aspects of data mining and data science, with an emphasis on controlling and mining dynamics of large scale networks. I also have strong interests in machine learning theory, especially in applying learning theory to accelerate data processing.
